The Rules

Dealing

To start the game, click on the start game button
This game uses only one deck with two hands of two cards dealt to the player and dealer
The goal of Blackjack is to get your hand as close to 21 without going over
You are playing against the dealer so whoever has the highest hand, without going bust (over 21), wins the hand!
Picture cards, Jack/Queen/King, count as 10 so a hand of 10 of Hearts and King of Spades will equate to 20
Aces can count as 11 or 1 depending on the sum of your hand, this is done automatically
If your hand consists of one card equal to 10 and an Ace you just got a Blackjack and instantly win the round!
Unless the dealer has a Blackjack too, in which case the hand is tied

Hit or Stand

Once the initial hands have been dealt, without a Blackjack, the game moves to phase two
The player now has a choice to hit or stand
Hit will add another card from the deck to your hand
You can hit as many times as you want but be careful, you don't want to go bust!
Once you're happy with your hand you can stand
