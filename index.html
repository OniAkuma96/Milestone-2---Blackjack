Rules

Dealing
  1. To start the game, click on the start game button
  2. The aim of Blackjack is to get your cards to add up to 21
  3. Jack/Queen/King count as 10
  4. Aces count as 1 or 11 depending on the sum of your hand
  5. If you have an ace and a card with a value of 10 that's a Blackjack and you win!
Hit or stand
  1. Once the initial hands have been dealt, without a Blackjack, the game moves to phase two
  2. You now have a choice to hit (add another card to your hand) or stand
  3. It's a good idea to look at the dealer's shown card and strategize
  4. Once you are happy with your hand you can stand and the winner is decided
Deciding the winner
  1. Before deciding the winner the dealer must hit or stand
  2. The dealer has to hit on 16
  3. Once the dealer has finished the player with the highest hand under 21 wins!
